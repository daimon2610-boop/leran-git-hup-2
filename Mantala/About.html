About

This website was created to offer a fresh and unique taste experience, designed especially for those who love exploring new flavors and discovering something different. Our goal is to bring together creativity and curiosity, allowing users to experience food in a new and exciting way. Whether you are a passionate food lover or simply someone looking for something new to try, this platform aims to inspire and introduce a variety of ideas, styles, and concepts related to food and taste. Please note that this is currently a test website and is not yet fully operational. Some features may still be under development, and we appreciate your patience as we continue to improve and expand the experience.

Production process

We have strict quality selection criteria, from the cricket breeds and feed to the rearing methods, ensuring the area is neither too hot, too humid, nor too cold.

Hitting the market

Our products will be distributed to general department stores and to groups of people who want to try or those who enjoy eating insects. (This is just a website project; no sales are happening yet.)
